Our Departments

Curation

Recruits our speakers and helps them prepare their talks and visuals for the conference.

  • Email-savvy and responsive
  • Strong writers
  • People-oriented collaborators

Curation is the backbone of our conference.

Finance

Our money masterminds keep the conference funded and on track.

  • Recruit sponsors (examples: Poppi, Bakelab, HERE Apartments)
  • Create and manage the conference budget
  • Coordinate with on-campus vendors

Marketing

Our social media and storytelling gurus build the TEDxUIUC voice.

  • Run Instagram and soon our website and additional socials
  • Design posts and campaigns (Canva skills welcome)
  • Draw inspiration from bold, witty brands

If you are creative and clever, marketing is your place.

Membership

The heart of our community that keeps members engaged and connected.

  • Plan bonding activities during meetings
  • Run recruitment in fall and spring
  • Partner with event planning to host socials and team-bonding events

Logistics + Ops

Super schedulers and organizers who keep everything moving smoothly.

  • Create the master schedule for the year
  • Reserve equipment and spaces (rooms, cameras, and more)
  • Keep the conference running seamlessly

If you are organized and a strong communicator, this is your team.

Technology & Production

The brains behind the screens and sound.

  • Manage all event technology, equipment, and live production
  • Record and coordinate high-quality video capture for archiving and TED submission
  • Ensure presentations, media, timing cues, and rehearsals run smoothly
  • Maintain and update the event website and digital assets

If you love tech, problem-solving, and live production, this is your team.

About TEDxUIUC

TEDxUIUC is a student-led initiative that brings the spirit of TED to the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. We unite changemakers, storytellers, and visionaries through powerful talks and creative collaboration.
